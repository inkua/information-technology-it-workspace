Human Front Aid

Our intention is to support projects regionally and locally.

People knowing the local conditions can help.

Our intention is to support projects regionally and locally. People knowing the local conditions can help effectively by adapting to the situation on ground immediately. They know best what is required at any moment, and we believe this is a reassurance that the resources are used in the best way possible.

We will continue supporting on-going projects as long as it’s needed. At the same time, we have all ears and eyes open for new ways of supporting as directly as we can, wherever it is. We find this crucial in order to stay open to new possibilities of helping in the most effective manner.
